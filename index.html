About This Tool

This app helps you find Kanji characters that match your desired meaning or concept. You can enter:

  • Simple words like "love" or "strength"
  • Phrases like "free spirited" or "inner peace"
  • Questions like "find me a Kanji for courage"
  • Descriptions like "a symbol that represents determination"

Understanding the Results

  • Green border: Perfect match (80%+ match) - Highly recommended for tattoos
  • Blue border: Good match (60-79% match) - Suitable for tattoos
  • Orange border: Warning - Has negative meanings related to your search

Warning System

Kanji often have multiple meanings and interpretations. Our tool will:

  • Show all possible meanings of a Kanji with their match percentages
  • Alert you with a warning if a Kanji has negative connotations that match your search
  • Provide an informational note when a Kanji has potential negative meanings

Always consult with a native Japanese speaker or professional tattoo artist before getting a Kanji tattoo. The meanings of Kanji can vary depending on context and combinations.
